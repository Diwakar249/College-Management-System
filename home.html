Admission Procedure

For admission to BBA/BCA candidates have to take a Common Entrance Test (CET) conducted by the institute every year in the month of June/July, followed by group discussion and personal interview. CET is a comprehensive test designed for 10+2 qualified candidates with objective questions on general English, quantitative aptitude, general knowledge and mental ability. 15% seats are reserved for NRI / Management sponsored candidates. ABOUT US

GENESIS & GROWTH OF TERI Technical Education and Research Institute was established as an appendage of Post-Graduate College, Ghazipur which has an illustrious history of nearly five decades. The college has been catering to the disparate academic needs of the society by offering a wide range of disciplines art, science, education, agricultural, science, music, environmental sciences, physical education etc. In 1994, TERI was the first and only institute to offers MCA degree programme from Veer Bahadur Singh Purvanchal University, Jaunpur.

VISION & MISSION

As the Indian economy is surging ahead, demand for technocrats is rising exponentially. But, the biggest problem is non availability of quality education, which leaves most of the tech graduates jobless even after having the degree. At TERI, we make extensive efforts to equip the students with key skills, which are critical for success at the corporate world. We have well qualified, experienced faculty to take care of education. The world class infrastructure at the campus ensures that the students do not miss a single learning opportunity.

TRAINING & PLACEMENT CELL

As a genuine provider of job-oriented education TERI gives a head start to its students through its full fledged training and placement Cell (TPC) which has significant recourses commitment in view of the severely competitive job environment. The TPC comprises of two training and placement officers, one each from the Department of Business Administration and the Department of Computer Application.

HOSTEL

HOSTEL The Institute provides comfortable residential living for boys and girls amidst lush green serene surroundings.
